DEVICE TRADE-IN PROGRAM GUIDE
==============================

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Get instant quote online or in-store
2. Order new device
3. Mail old device with prepaid label (provided)
4. We inspect and verify condition
5. Credit applied to account

ACCEPTED DEVICES:
✓ Smartphones (all brands)
✓ Tablets (Apple, Samsung, Microsoft)
✓ Smartwatches (Apple, Samsung, Garmin)
✓ Basic phones
✓ Hotspots

CONDITION REQUIREMENTS:

GOOD CONDITION (Full Value):
- Powers on and off
- No cracks on screen or body
- All buttons work
- No water damage
- Find My iPhone/Activation Lock disabled

FAIR CONDITION (50% Value):
- Minor screen cracks
- Some buttons don't work
- Light water exposure
- Still powers on

POOR CONDITION (25% Value):
- Won't power on
- Severe damage
- Major water damage
- Still accepted for recycling credit
