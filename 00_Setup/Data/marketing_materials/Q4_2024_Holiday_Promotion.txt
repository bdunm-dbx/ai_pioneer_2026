HOLIDAY 2024 PROMOTIONAL CAMPAIGN
=====================================

CAMPAIGN NAME: "Gift of Connection"
DURATION: November 15, 2024 - January 15, 2025
TARGET: New and existing customers
BUDGET: $8.5M (media + incentives)
OWNER: Holiday Marketing Task Force

---
CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW
---

Theme: Connecting families during the holiday season
Key Message: Give the gift of staying connected with loved ones
Primary CTA: "Unwrap the savings"

Objectives:
1. Acquire 150,000 new lines (goal: 12% increase over Q4 2023)
2. Drive device upgrades (target: $45M device revenue)
3. Increase family plan conversions by 25%
4. Boost brand sentiment during competitive holiday period

---
PROMOTIONAL OFFERS
---

**OFFER 1: New Line Holiday Special**
- Add a new line, get $200 bill credit
- Requires port-in from another carrier
- Credit applied over 8 months ($25/month)
- Valid on Unlimited Plus or Premium plans
- Limit 4 new lines per account

**OFFER 2: Device Trade-In Bonus**
- Up to $800 trade-in value on select devices
- $100 additional holiday bonus credit
- Applies to iPhone 15 series, Samsung Galaxy S24 series, Google Pixel 8 series
- Device must be in good working condition
- Trade-in device must be received within 30 days

**OFFER 3: Family Plan Deal**
- 4 lines for $100/month (Unlimited Basic)
- Save $60/month vs. individual lines
- Includes unlimited talk, text, data
- 5GB hotspot per line
- First month FREE for new customers

**OFFER 4: Free Streaming Services**
- 6 months free Apple Music (valued at $60)
- 3 months free Apple TV+ (valued at $30)
- Available with Unlimited Plus or Premium plans
- Auto-renews after promotional period unless cancelled

**OFFER 5: Device Accessory Bundle**
- Free wireless charger + case + screen protector
- With any device purchase $699 or more
- Bundle value: $120
- Limited quantities, while supplies last

---
TARGET SEGMENTS
---

1. **Family Planners** (Primary)
   - Age: 35-55
   - Current: 2-3 lines, considering adding more
   - Pain point: High costs for multiple lines
   - Message: "More lines, more savings"

2. **Gift Givers** (Secondary)
   - Age: 25-65
   - Looking for meaningful gifts
   - Pain point: Finding tech gifts that make sense
   - Message: "The gift that keeps them connected all year"

3. **Switchers** (Tertiary)
   - Age: 25-45
   - Unhappy with current carrier
   - Pain point: Price or service quality
   - Message: "Switch and save this holiday season"

4. **Upgraders** (Opportunity)
   - Age: 18-65
   - Device 2+ years old
   - Pain point: Old device performance
   - Message: "Trade up to the latest tech"

---
COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS
---

Anticipated competitor moves:
- Carrier A: Likely "Free iPhone" promotion (requires 36-month commitment)
- Carrier B: Expected BOGO deals on mid-tier devices
- Carrier C: May offer 50% off switching incentives

Our differentiation:
- Shorter commitment periods (24 months vs 36)
- Transparent pricing (taxes/fees disclosed upfront)
- No "gotcha" fine print
- Superior network performance
