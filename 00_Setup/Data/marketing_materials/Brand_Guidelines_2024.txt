VISUAL IDENTITY
---

Primary Brand Colors:
- Signature Blue: #0066CC (RGB: 0, 102, 204)
- Accent Orange: #FF6600 (RGB: 255, 102, 0)
- Neutral Gray: #4A4A4A (RGB: 74, 74, 74)
- White: #FFFFFF
- Black: #000000

Color Usage:
- Signature Blue: Primary brand color for logos, headers, CTAs
- Accent Orange: Highlights, promotions, urgency messaging
- Neutral Gray: Body text, secondary elements
- Use white or black for contrast and readability

Typography:
- Headlines: "Gotham Bold" or "Montserrat Bold" (web-safe alternative)
- Body Copy: "Open Sans Regular" or "Helvetica Neue"
- Minimum font size: 12pt for print, 16px for digital

Logo Usage:
- Minimum clear space: Equal to height of "T" in logo on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or alter logo proportions
- Never place logo on busy backgrounds without white/dark overlay
- Approved logo variations: Full color, white, black, horizontal, stacked

BRAND VOICE & TONE
---

Voice Characteristics:
- Confident but not arrogant
- Friendly but professional
- Clear and jargon-free
- Helpful and solution-oriented

Tone Variations by Channel:
- Customer Service: Empathetic, patient, solution-focused
- Marketing: Energetic, benefit-driven, aspirational
- Technical: Clear, educational, precise
- Social Media: Conversational, responsive, authentic

Language Guidelines:
DO: Use "you" and "your" (customer-centric language)
DO: Lead with benefits before features
DO: Keep sentences short and scannable
DON'T: Use telecom jargon without explanation
DON'T: Make promises we can't keep
DON'T: Use all caps (except for required legal text)

COMPLIANCE NOTES
---

All marketing materials must include:
- Clear pricing with tax/fee disclosures
- Terms and conditions for promotional offers
- Required regulatory language for specific claims
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)

Restricted Claims:
- Do not claim "fastest" without current third-party verification
- Do not claim "best coverage" without qualifying geography
- Do not use competitor names in negative comparisons
- Always substantiate technical performance claims
